About Our Program

A comprehensive, industry-focused design education program that transforms beginners into confident UI/UX professionals through structured learning and hands-on practice.

Program Overview

The Kala-Klub Figma EDU Bootcamp is an intensive 12-week program designed to transform aspiring designers into industry-ready professionals. Our structured approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, ensuring graduates have both the skills and portfolio needed to succeed in competitive design roles.

Unlike traditional online courses, our bootcamp provides personalized mentorship, peer collaboration, and real-world project experience. We focus specifically on Figma mastery while building a strong foundation in UI/UX principles, design thinking, and professional workflow practices.

Assessment & Certification

Our assessment approach focuses on practical application and real-world skills rather than traditional testing. Students are evaluated on their ability to apply design principles, solve problems creatively, and present their work professionally.

Continuous Assessment (60%)

  • Weekly Assignments: Practical exercises that build on each lesson
  • Peer Reviews: Collaborative evaluation and feedback sessions
  • Participation: Active engagement in discussions and workshops
  • Progress Milestones: Regular check-ins with mentors

Portfolio Projects (40%)

  • Individual Projects: Three progressive assignments (10% each)
  • Capstone Project: Comprehensive final project (10%)
  • Presentation: Professional presentation of all work
  • Reflection: Written analysis of learning journey
