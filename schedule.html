Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join if I have a full-time job?

Absolutely! Our schedule is designed for working professionals. Evening classes and weekend reviews accommodate most work schedules.

What if I miss a live session?

All sessions are recorded and available within 24 hours. You can catch up and still participate in the next session.

Are there makeup sessions?

We offer quarterly makeup workshops for students who need additional support or miss critical sessions.

What's the time commitment?

Plan for 15-20 hours per week: 6 hours live sessions + 8-10 hours project work + 4-6 hours self-study.

Can I switch cohorts?

Yes, if you need to pause for personal reasons, you can join the next available cohort within 12 months at no extra cost.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a full refund within the first week if the program isn't the right fit. Partial refunds available until week 3.
