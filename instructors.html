About Gaurav

Gaurav Kumar is the visionary founder of Kala-Klub, bringing over 8 years of experience in software development and design education. As an Integration Specialist, he has worked with numerous organizations to streamline their design and development workflows, making him uniquely qualified to teach the intersection of design and technology.

His passion for education emerged from his own journey of transitioning from traditional software development to modern product design. Gaurav recognized the gap between academic design education and industry requirements, leading him to create Kala-Klub as a bridge for aspiring designers.

Expertise & Specializations

  • Design Systems Architecture: Building scalable design systems for enterprise applications
  • Developer Collaboration: Streamlining design-to-development handoff processes
  • Tool Integration: Optimizing workflows between Figma, development tools, and project management systems
  • Mentorship & Leadership: Guiding design teams and individual career development

Professional Experience

  • Integration Specialist at multiple tech companies, focusing on design-development workflows
  • Freelance consultant for startups and established businesses seeking design process optimization
  • Workshop facilitator for corporate design teams and educational institutions
  • Open source contributor to design tools and educational resources

💡 Teaching Philosophy

Our Teaching Approach

🎓 Structured Learning Path

Our curriculum is carefully designed to build skills progressively, ensuring each concept builds on previous knowledge while introducing new challenges.

📝 Practical Assignments

Every lesson includes hands-on exercises that reinforce learning and provide immediate application opportunities for new concepts.

🔄 Continuous Feedback

Regular one-on-one sessions and group critiques ensure students receive personalized guidance throughout their learning journey.

🤝 Collaborative Learning

Students work in teams on real projects, simulating professional environments and building essential collaboration skills.

🎯 Goal-Oriented Sessions

Each session has clear objectives and outcomes, helping students track their progress and understand their growth.

🌟 Career Guidance

Beyond technical skills, we provide guidance on portfolio development, interview preparation, and career planning.
